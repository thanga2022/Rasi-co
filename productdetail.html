Description Nutritional Info Usage

Discover the ancient goodness of Ragi, also known as Finger Millet. Our flour is sourced from certified organic farms and is stone-ground to preserve its natural nutrients and flavor. It is an excellent source of calcium, iron, and dietary fiber, making it a perfect addition to a healthy diet.

  • 100% Organic: Free from pesticides and harmful chemicals.
  • Gluten-Free: A great alternative for those with gluten sensitivities.
  • Rich in Fiber: Promotes digestive health and keeps you full longer.
  • High in Calcium: Essential for strong bones and teeth.

Use it to make soft rotis, crispy dosas, nutritious porridge, or bake it into healthy cookies and cakes. Its earthy flavor adds a unique taste to your dishes.
